# Northstar Health Analytics — Company Context

### Section 1. Company Overview

**1.1 Organisation Name.** Northstar Health Analytics is a growth-stage health technology organisation based in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

**1.2 Business Focus.** The organisation operates at the intersection of health data infrastructure and analytics services, supporting healthcare systems, research bodies, and public health organisations across Europe.

**1.3 Growth Stage.** The company has grown from twelve to over forty employees in the past three years and is currently preparing to expand into two additional European markets within the next twelve months.

**1.4 Operational Structure.** The organisation operates with a lean structure, limited managerial layers, and limited slack capacity. Staff are expected to work independently, manage cross-functional coordination, and maintain operational reliability without heavy supervision.

### Section 2. Talent Operations Context

**2.1 Hiring Volume.** The organisation is preparing for a structured intake over the coming period, with multiple roles being added across operations, analytics, and client services.

**2.2 Current Operational Gap.** There is currently no dedicated HR or talent operations function. Hiring and people-related processes are managed across leadership and team leads, creating coordination overhead and procedural inconsistency.

**2.3 Strategic Purpose of the Hire.** The role being advertised is intended to establish a scalable and reliable talent operations function within the organisation, covering structured screening support, candidate communication, and workflow management.

**2.4 Team Context.** The person in this role will work directly with leadership, department leads, and external candidates to ensure that hiring processes are clear, trackable, and executed consistently.

### Section 3. Evaluation Standards

**3.1 Hiring Philosophy.** The organisation values operational reliability, structured coordination, and procedural discipline over formal title matches or narrow experience criteria.

**3.2 Candidate Expectations.** Candidates are expected to demonstrate they can manage multi-step coordination processes, handle stakeholder communication independently, and maintain workflow discipline in a fast-moving SME setting.

**3.3 Transferable Experience Welcome.** The organisation recognises that prior experience may come from adjacent roles in non-profit, public sector, or SME settings where formal recruitment titles were not used but equivalent coordination and process work was performed.
